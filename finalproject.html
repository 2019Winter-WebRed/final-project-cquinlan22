Biography

Misty Copeland was born on Setember 10th, 1982 in Kansas City Missouri. Even though Misty is considred a dance prodigy she didn't begin dancing until she was thirteen. She was born into a troubled family, and when she was 15 her dance instructors fought a custody war for her. Later, she moved back home and continued to study dance, but under a new instructor at ABT the third leading dance studio in the country to become a soloist in 2007. On June 30th, 2015 Misty became the fist African American woman to become the principal dancer of ABT.

Influence

Misty Copeland has had a huge influence on others. She is one of the biggest representations of the African American race in te ballet community.
